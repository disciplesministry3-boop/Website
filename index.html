Our Vision

We believe in empowering people in seeking God individually and corporately to develop a vision for the future as we grow in effectiveness and excellence in the gospel. By building more churches in rural Africa and distributing Bibles and religious books to African prisons and people living in severe poverty.

Things We Do

  • Build churches in the most remote parts of Africa.
  • Nurture and grow children spiritually in Christ.
  • Consistent international prayer.
  • Prison ministry.
  • Community outreach.

Our Pillars

Christ-Centered

Jesus is the Head of the Church, and all that we do is guided by the will of God through the power of the Holy Spirit (Ephesians 1:22–23).

Love

Love is at the heart of who God is. We reflect this love by serving others sacrificially, just as Christ served us (John 3:16).

Hope

We carry confident expectation and trust in God, even when the path ahead is not visible.

Faith

We walk by faith, trusting God’s promises, not merely what we can see (2 Corinthians 5:7).

Kingdom-Minded

We proclaim the Gospel, make disciples, and establish strong, indigenous communities of believers.

Why This Mission Matters

Growing up, many of us had to walk 30 minutes to over an hour just to reach a place of worship. Along the way, we faced real dangers, including encounters with deadly wildlife. I personally knew a friend who was bitten by a black mamba during one of these journeys.

No one should have to risk their life to worship. That experience is why Disciples of Hope International Ministry exists to bring churches closer to communities, reduce these dangers, and ensure that faith is accessible to everyone.
